How to play

Fill every white square so that the across and down answers match their numbered clues.

Click a square to select it; click again to switch between Across and Down. The highlighted clue shows in the yellow bar.

Type letters to fill, Backspace to delete, arrow keys to move, Tab for the next clue, Space to toggle direction.

Stuck? Use Assist to check or reveal a letter, word, or the whole grid. Turn on Error check mode in Settings to mark wrong letters automatically.

Solve the whole grid correctly to win! Press Esc or click outside this box to close it.
